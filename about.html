About Travis Scott

Travis Scott, or Jacques Berman Webster II, is one of the most popular American rappers. Already nominated for eight Grammy Awards and having won a Billboard Music Award, Travis continues to change the game with his new music. Not only does his music revolutionize the industry, but he continues to find new ways to reach his audience, such as on a video game like Fortnite. After his performances in April in 2020 from which he gained nearly 27 million viewers, many began to start considering concert to become virtual events, paving the way for a new way to enjoy music.

What is Astronomical 2

Astronomical 2 is a concert in the form of a virtual event on the popular game Fortnite. Fortnite is a battle royale third person shooter where players can play with friends or or their own in various different game modes including solos, duos, four person squads, or even massive 50v50 rumbles. When concerts were first shut down due to Covid, Travis found a way to perform and reach his audience virtually, on Fortnite. An virtual concert on a video game of this level was something never seen before, and revolutionized the game. Now Travis wishes to continue to reach his audience in this unique way as it was extremely successful last time in The Original Astronomical.
