Learn more about the original

On April 23, 2020, Travis Scott and Fortnite held the first Astronomical. Viewed by nearly 28 million people across the five showing, a truly Astronomical event took place. The concert included popular hits such as "Sicko Mode", "Stargazing", "Goosebumps", "Highest in the Room" and even a new song, "The Scotts"

A video showing the event is shown. As seen, many players came in with friends or on their own and got the experience of a concert virtually! With the ability to dance, talk and most importantly listen to music, Astronomical provided fans with a concert they could not have due to Covid.
